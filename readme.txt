== Description ==

Alt Text Generator AI is an intuitive WordPress plugin designed to streamline the process of generating image alt texts for your website. With a simple click, this plugin scans your media library, identifies images without alt text, and uses advanced AI computer vision technology to create descriptive, SEO-friendly alt texts.

### Key Features ###

- **One-click Alt Text Generation**: Easily scan your website and generate alt texts with a single click.
- **Automated Scanning**: Continuously monitors your media library for new uploads.
- **Bulk Alt Text Generation**: Bulk generate alt texts for all media library images at once.
- **Review and Edit**: Maintain complete control over your content by reviewing and editing the generated alt texts as needed.
- **Pay As You Go Pricing**: No monthly subscriptions. Just pay for the images you optimize, starting with 10 free credits.
- **State-of-the-Art Technology**: Leverage our advanced AI model, trained on millions of images, to ensure high accuracy and industry-leading performance in alt text generation.

Alt Text Generator AI is specifically designed for WordPress, guaranteeing easy installation and compatibility with your existing images. It’s not just a tool; it’s a game-changer for enhancing your website’s SEO and accessibility!

For more details and advanced features, visit our official website: [https://alttextgenerator.ai](https://alttextgenerator.ai)

== Installation ==

= Steps to install the plugin =

To install the plugin, follow the below steps:

Step 1: Log in to your WordPress dashboard. 
Step 2: Navigate to Plugins and select Add New. 
Step 3: In the search bar, type “Alt Text Generator AI” and click Search Plugins. 
Step 4: Once you find the AI Alt Text Generator plugin by WebToffee, click on “Install Now”.
Step 5: After installation, click “Activate” to activate the plugin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Alt Text? =

Alt text (alternative text) is a brief description of an image used within the HTML of a website. It's crucial for website accessibility, especially for visually impaired users who rely on screen readers. Alt text also plays a pivotal role in SEO, helping search engines understand the content of images.

= How does the AI model generate alt texts? =

Our AI alt text generator uses an advanced Computer Vision model trained on millions of images. It analyzes each image to produce descriptive alt texts, ensuring accuracy and SEO relevance.

= How is the pricing structured? =

We offer a pay-as-you-go pricing model. You start with 10 free credits, and after that, you can purchase additional credits as needed. Each credit corresponds to an image optimized.

= Can I manually edit the generated alt texts? =

Yes, you can! Our AI model strives for precision, but you have the freedom to review, modify, or enhance the generated alt texts whenever necessary.
